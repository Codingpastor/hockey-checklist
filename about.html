About Hockey Gear Check

The story behind the checklist that saves hockey parents' sanity

Our Story

Hockey Gear Check began with a simple yet all-too-common problem: forgotten hockey equipment.

As a hockey parent, I experienced the stress of arriving at the rink only to discover my child was missing shin pads, a mouthguard, or a water bottle. These moments led to frantic last-minute purchases, borrowing from other families, or worst of all, having to tell my excited young player they couldn't participate.

After one particularly chaotic morning that involved two separate trips between home and the rink, I decided there had to be a better way. That's when Hockey Gear Check was born - a simple, free, and effective way to ensure no piece of hockey equipment is ever left behind again.

Built by a hockey parent for hockey parents, this tool is designed to make the hockey experience more enjoyable for everyone involved - players, parents, and coaches alike.

How It Works

Hockey Gear Check is designed to be as simple as possible:

  1. Open the checklist on any device before packing for hockey
  2. Select player or goalie mode depending on your needs
  3. Check off each item as you pack it
  4. Enjoy the satisfaction of seeing your progress bar reach 100%
  5. Head to the rink with confidence, knowing nothing was forgotten

The app uses your browser's local storage to remember your progress, but automatically resets each time you visit so you can start fresh for each game or practice.

FAQ

Do I need to create an account?

No! Hockey Gear Check is designed to be used without any registration or login. Just open the website and start using it.

Does this work on all devices?

Yes, Hockey Gear Check works on smartphones, tablets, and computers. Use whatever device is most convenient for you.

Why does the checklist reset each time?

The checklist resets for each new visit to ensure you're starting fresh for each game or practice. This prevents confusion about what was packed for today versus a previous event.
